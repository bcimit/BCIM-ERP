Inspection Checklists & Forms

Digital inspection forms tied to construction phases. Inspectors fill checklists on-site via mobile, attach evidence, and submit for review — replacing paper-based processes entirely.

🏗️ Phase-Based Templates

Pre-built inspection templates for every construction phase: Site Preparation, Foundation, Structural, MEP (Mechanical/Electrical/Plumbing), Waterproofing, Finishing, and Handover.

Core

🛠️ Custom Checklist Builder

Drag-and-drop form builder to create custom checklists. Add yes/no items, numeric measurements, dropdown selectors, text fields, and mandatory photo items.

Core

✅ Auto-Scoring & Verdicts

Automatic pass/fail/conditional scoring based on item weights. Configurable thresholds per project type (residential vs. commercial) with override capability for authorized reviewers.

Core

📲 Mobile Field Entry

Responsive, touch-optimized interface for tablets and phones. Large buttons, swipe navigation between items, quick photo attachment, and voice-to-text for comments.

Core

🔗 NCR Auto-Generation

When a checklist item fails, automatically prompt creation of an NCR with pre-filled data (location, trade, item description, photo). Reduces duplicate data entry.

Advanced

👥 Multi-Party Sign-Off

Digital signature capture for inspector, contractor, and client sign-off. Tracks who signed, when, and any conditional remarks attached to approval.

Core

📊 Inspection Schedule

Calendar view of planned inspections tied to project milestones. Auto-reminders for upcoming inspections with status tracking (Pending, In Progress, Completed, Overdue).

Advanced

📄 PDF Export

One-click export of completed inspection reports as branded PDF documents with company logo, photos, signatures, and compliance stamps. Suitable for client or authority submissions.

Core
⚠️

Non-Conformance Reports (NCR) & Defect Tracking

End-to-end NCR lifecycle from detection to closeout. Categorize, assign, track corrective actions, and analyze root causes to prevent recurrence across projects.

📝 NCR Creation & Logging

Quick-entry NCR form with auto-numbering (e.g., NCR-2026-001). Capture: description, location (floor/zone/grid), trade/subcontractor, specification reference, and affected drawing number.

Core

🔴 Severity Classification

Three-tier severity system: Critical (stop work), Major (must fix before next phase), Minor (punch list). Configurable escalation rules and response deadlines per severity level.

Core

🔄 NCR Workflow Engine

Status pipeline: Open → Under Review → Corrective Action → Verification → Closed. Configurable workflows per project. Automatic notifications at each stage transition.

Core

👷 Assignment & Accountability

Assign NCRs to specific subcontractors or team members. Track response times, overdue items, and contractor performance scores based on NCR resolution metrics.

Core

🔍 Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

Built-in RCA tools: 5-Why method, Fishbone/Ishikawa diagram, and categorized cause types (Material, Workmanship, Design, Method, Environment). Links findings to preventive actions.

Advanced

📋 Corrective Action Report (CAR)

Formal CAR form attached to NCRs with: proposed fix, responsible party, target date, actual completion date, and verification evidence. Supports re-inspection workflows.

Core

📊 NCR Analytics Dashboard

Real-time metrics: NCRs by trade, by severity, by location, aging reports, recurrence trends, and top offending contractors. Exportable charts for management review meetings.

Advanced

📄 NCR Report Generator

Generate formal NCR reports with full history: initial finding, photos, RCA, corrective actions taken, verification evidence, and sign-off. Branded PDF ready for submittal.

Core

🔗 Linked NCR Registry

Central NCR register with advanced filtering, search, and cross-referencing. Link related NCRs to identify systemic issues. Export full register as Excel or PDF for audits.

Integration
📁

Document Control & Approvals

Centralized document management with revision control, approval workflows, and distribution tracking. Ensures every team member works from the latest approved documents.

📂 Document Repository

Organized folder structure by project, discipline, and type (Drawings, Specs, ITP, Method Statements, Material Submittals). Full-text search across all uploaded documents.

Core

🔄 Revision Control

Automatic version tracking with revision numbering (Rev A, B, C…). Side-by-side revision comparison. Superseded documents clearly marked with access to full revision history.

Core

✅ Approval Workflows

Multi-level approval chains: Prepare → Review → Approve → Issue. Configurable approvers per document type. Track approval status, comments, and revision requests in one timeline.

Core

📤 Transmittal Management

Generate transmittal sheets for document distribution. Track who received what, when, and acknowledgment status. Auto-log all transmittals for audit trail compliance.

Core

📐 Drawing Register

Dedicated drawing register with status tracking per drawing (For Construction, For Approval, As-Built). Link drawings to inspection locations and NCRs for full traceability.

Advanced

📝 Material Submittal Tracker

Track material submittals from submission to approval. Log vendor data, spec compliance, test certificates, and approval stamps. Alert for pending submittals blocking work.

Advanced

🔒 Access Control

Role-based document access. Control who can view, download, edit, or approve documents. Watermark confidential documents. Full download/view audit log.

Core
📷

Photo Evidence & Markup

Capture, annotate, and organize site photographs as verifiable quality evidence. Every photo is timestamped, geo-tagged, and linked to the relevant inspection or NCR.

📸 Smart Photo Capture

In-app camera with auto-timestamp, GPS location, and project/zone tagging. Batch upload from gallery with bulk-tagging. Supports HEIC, JPEG, and PNG formats.

Core

✏️ Markup & Annotation

Draw arrows, circles, rectangles, and free-form annotations on photos. Add text labels, measurement callouts, and severity color coding. Save original + annotated versions.

Core

🔁 Before / After Comparison

Side-by-side and slider comparison view for defect-and-fix documentation. Link "before" photos from NCR creation to "after" photos from corrective action verification.

Advanced

🗂️ Smart Albums

Auto-organized albums by project, floor, zone, inspection date, and NCR number. Quick filters and search by tag, date range, or linked record. Gallery and list views.

Core

🔗 Cross-Linking

Every photo links back to its parent inspection or NCR. View all photos related to a specific defect, location, or trade from one unified timeline.

Core

📄 Photo Report Builder

Generate professional photo reports with captions, location info, and timestamps. Customizable layouts (2-up, 4-up, full page). Export as PDF for client submittals.

Advanced
📈

Dashboard & Reporting

Real-time quality intelligence at your fingertips. Role-specific dashboards for QC managers, project managers, and executives with exportable reports for all stakeholders.

📊 Quality KPI Dashboard

At-a-glance metrics: Inspection pass rate, open NCR count, average resolution time, defect density per zone, and quality score trending. Auto-refreshing real-time data.

Core

⏱️ NCR Aging Report

Visual aging breakdown of open NCRs: 0-7 days, 7-14 days, 14-30 days, 30+ days. Highlight critical overdue items. Drill down to individual NCRs from any aging bucket.

Core

📈 Trend Analysis

Weekly/monthly quality trends with trendlines. Identify recurring defect types, problem trades, and high-risk zones. Predictive alerts for quality deterioration patterns.

Advanced

👷 Contractor Scorecard

Performance scorecards per subcontractor: NCR count, resolution speed, first-time pass rate, and overall quality rating. Use for prequalification decisions on future projects.

Advanced

📄 Report Generator

Build custom reports with drag-and-drop widgets. Weekly quality report, monthly management summary, project closeout quality dossier. Schedule automated email delivery.

Core

👤 Role-Based Views

QC Inspector sees their assigned inspections/NCRs. Project Manager sees project-wide quality health. Executive sees portfolio-level metrics. Each role gets relevant data only.

Core

🔔 Smart Notifications

Configurable alerts: NCR assigned to you, inspection overdue, approval pending, critical NCR raised, document revision issued. Push, email, and in-app notification channels.
